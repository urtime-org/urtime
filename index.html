    ✨ About UrTime

    UrTime is your personal productivity companion built to help you focus, finish tasks, and feel accomplished — one Pomodoro at a time. 🚀🌱🫧

    🌱 Grow as You Focus

    Every minute you stay focused, your plant grows! It visually tracks your discipline and motivates you to stay on task until the timer ends.

    🚀 Launch with Progress

    Start a session and your rocket shakes with excitement. When you finish, it zooms into the sky — just like your goals!

    🫧 Breathe and Flow

    Select the Bubbles theme for a calm, ambient atmosphere. Perfect for deep work and gentle focus sessions.

    🎵 Personalized Vibes

    Choose from lofi beats, nature sounds, or café ambience — music runs only during focus time to keep your mind engaged.

    📝 Smart To-Do List

    Add your daily tasks, check them off as you go, and stay organized. Mark tasks as complete with a single click! Bonus: UrTime's built-in AI suggests YouTube videos or resources based on what you're working on — perfect for quick learning!

    📅 Dashboard Calendar

    Log in to track your productivity every day. View your progress, completed Pomodoros, minutes focused, and tasks done — all neatly organized by date.
